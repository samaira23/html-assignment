Computer science and technology

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems, encompassing both theoretical and practical aspects. It involves the design, development, and analysis of algorithms, software, and hardware.

Key areas of computer science include programming, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, data structures, computer networks, and human-computer interaction. This field drives innovation across various industries by creating efficient solutions to complex problems, enabling advancements in technology, and transforming how we process, store, and communicate information. Computer science is fundamental to the modern digital world, influencing everything from everyday applications to cutting-edge research and development.
